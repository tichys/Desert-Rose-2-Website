A Breif History of the Pre-War Fallout Universe:

  • April 2052: The Resource Wars begin
  • January 2054: Construction of the Vaults for Project Safehouse begins
  • Winter 2066: China invades Alaska starting the Battle of Anchorage
  • January 2077: The Battle of Anchorage ends with Alaska back in US hands
  • October 23, 2077: 12:03am(EST) The Great War begins
  • October 23, 2077: 9:47am(EST) The "Great War" ends sending the world into Nuclear winter

History of the Fallout Universe:

  • December 5, 2161: The events of "Fallout" begin with the Vault Dweller leaving Vault 13 in search for a replacement water chip
  • May 10, 2162: The events of "Fallout" conclude as the Vault Dweller returns to Vault 13.
  • July 5, 2241:The events of "Fallout 2" begin as "The Chosen One" prepares for his journey to find a "Garden of Eden Creation Kit"
  • Fall of 2242 "Fallout 2" concludes with the Chosen One returning to his home
  • August 17, 2277:The events of "Fallout 3" begins as the Lone Wanderer leaves Vault 101 in search of his father
  • 2278: The events of "Fallout 3" conluced with the Lone Wanderer having saved the Capital Wastland.
  • October 19, 2281: The events of "Fallout: New Vegas" begin with the Courier setting out across the Mojave to seek revenge.
  • 2282: The events of Fallout:New Vegas conclude with the Courier having decided the Fate of the Mojave.
  • October 23,2287: The events of "Fallout 4" begin, the Sole Survivor leaves Vault 111 in search of their son Shaun, traveling across the Commmonwealth.
  • 2288: The events of Fallout 4 conclude, the Sole Survivor having decided the fate of the Commonwealth.
